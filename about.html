Dr. PRADEEP KANCHAN

Dr. Pradeep Kanchan has been awarded the PhD Degree from National Institute of Technology Karnataka (NITK), Surathkal in July 2022 for his Thesis entitled “Improved Nature Inspired Algorithms for Optimization problems in Wireless Sensor Networks”. Dr.Pradeep studied in Little Rock Indian School from classes 8 to 12. He completed his BE in Computer Science and Engineering from NMAMIT, Nitte in the year 1999, MTech from NITK, Surathkal in 2008 and now PhD also from NITK, Surathkal in 2022.

He is also a Senior Member of IEEE. The Project ”Food Vending Machine for Schools” of BE students guided by him got an award of appreciation at Gandhian Young Technological Innovation Awards 2015 as part of ‘Festival of Innovation’ held at Rashtrapathi Bhavan, New Delhi in March 2015.
